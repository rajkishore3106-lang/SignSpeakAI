About SignSpeak AI

Project Overview

SignSpeak AI is an Artificial Intelligence based web application that converts Sign Language into Text and Speech using Computer Vision, MediaPipe and TensorFlow.


Objectives

  • ✔ Detect hand gestures in real time
  • ✔ Convert Sign Language into text
  • ✔ Convert text into speech
  • ✔ Help deaf and mute people communicate easily
